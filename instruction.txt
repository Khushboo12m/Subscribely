Every minute, Laravel’s scheduler checks if there’s any job that’s due.

When the time matches (once a day in our case), it runs your command:
That command finds subscriptions due in 3 days and sends reminders.

# php artisan make:command SendSubscriptionReminders


Try this command manually (it simulates the scheduler):
# php artisan schedule:run
